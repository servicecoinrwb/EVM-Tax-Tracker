EVM Crypto Tax Worksheet

US capital-gains worksheet · wallet-by-wallet FIFO · Ethereum + Arbitrum · Form 8949 / Schedule D draft
This is a worksheet, not tax advice. It does a first-pass classification of on-chain activity and computes FIFO gains. DeFi positions (Aave, GMX, LP tokens, vault deposits) are genuinely ambiguous under current IRS guidance and frequently mis-handled by every automated tool — you confirm those in the Review step. Reconcile against any 1099-DA you receive and have a CPA review before filing.

1Connect

Keys stay in this browser only (localStorage). Get a free Etherscan V2 key at etherscan.io/apis — one key covers both chains. CoinGecko demo key is optional but raises the price-lookup rate limit.

2Your wallets

Add every address you control. Transfers between these (including bridging ETH→Arbitrum) are treated as non-taxable moves that carry cost basis. Anything to/from an address not listed here is treated as an external party.

Add many at once

Paste addresses — one per line, or comma/space separated. Pasting a list with extra text is fine; it pulls out every 0x… address, labels them wallet 1, 2, 3…, and skips duplicates.

3Pull on-chain history

Fetches full history (all years) so cost basis is correct, across Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, BNB Chain and Polygon. Many wallets across six chains can take several minutes on a free key. Re-running merges with what's cached.
